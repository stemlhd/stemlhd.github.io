More About

LEARN

Local Hack Day: Learn is where you pick up new skills by experiencing a professional conference first-hand. Attendees will participate in a series of workshops in which they'll learn enjoyable and helpful skills such as publishing their first website, sending their first automatic message, and, of course, others!

BUILD

Local Hack Day: Build is where you get first-hand experience bringing their ideas to life by participating in a hackathon. attendees will use hardware and software to build everything from websites to robots to mobile apps and more.

SHARE

Local Hack Day: Share is where you highlight the accomplishments of your work. Put the final touches on your projects and develop your communication skills by participating in this demo day. Attendees will spend time writing and speaking about the things they learned and built during the last year. At the end of the event, they'll have shared their ideas with the world.
